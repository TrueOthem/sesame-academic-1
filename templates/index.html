About SESAME Academic

SESAME Academic is a comprehensive tool for sustainability analysis in energy systems. It provides capabilities for:

  • Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) to evaluate environmental impacts
  • Techno-Economic Analysis (TEA) to assess economic feasibility
  • Pathway Analysis to explore different energy production and consumption routes
  • Fleet Analysis for transportation systems
  • Grid Analysis for electricity systems
  • Industry-specific analyses for cement, steel, and aluminum production
